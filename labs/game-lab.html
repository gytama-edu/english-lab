How to Play Top Answer Showdown

Top Answer Showdown is a teacher-hosted team game.

  1. Setup: Divide the class into two teams. The teacher controls the website.
  2. Face-off: The teacher listens to one answer from each team. Type the answers into the host controls to check if they match any hidden answers. Decide a face-off winner who becomes the active team or passes to the other team.
  3. Active Play: The active team tries to guess the remaining hidden answers. The teacher types the answers. Correct answers reveal points which are added to the round bank.
  4. Strikes: If a guess is incorrect, the teacher marks a strike. After exactly three strikes, the other team tries to steal.
  5. Steal Attempt: The stealing team gives one answer. If it matches a hidden answer, they take the round bank. If it doesn't, the original active team keeps it.
  6. Scoring: At the end of the round, the team that secured the round bank earns the points multiplied by the round's multiplier.

Note: The points shown on the answer board are authored game points created for this activity. They are not real survey statistics.
